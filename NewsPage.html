March 31, 2025
UCC Taekwondo North & South Campus Dual Meet!
The University of Caloocan City's North and South campus Taekwondo Club held a dual meet, focusing on kyorugi and poomsae to select athletes for the upcoming Local Colleges and Universities Athletic Association (LCUAA) competition. The event highlighted skill, discipline, and sportsmanship as UCC's fighters showcased their best techniques.
COMING SOON!
UCC Taekwondo Club North Campus Summer Training!
The University of Caloocan City Taekwondo Club is preparing for its summer training program, aimed at refining athletes' skills in kyorugi and poomsae. While the official schedule is yet to be announced, participants can expect intensive sessions designed to enhance technique, discipline, and competitive readiness.
