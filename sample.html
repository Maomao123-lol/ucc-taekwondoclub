TRAINING

WHO CAN JOIN?

All students, from any program and year, are welcome to join our Taekwondo training. Whether you are a beginner or experienced, this is your chance to develop strength, discipline, and confidence in a supportive environment. Train with skilled instructors, enhance your abilities, and become part of a dynamic community. Do not miss this opportunity—join us and unlock your full potential.
Lahat ng estudyante, mula sa anumang programa at taon, ay malugod na inaanyayahan na sumali sa aming pagsasanay sa Taekwondo. Maging ikaw ay baguhan o may karanasan na, ito ang pagkakataon mong mapaunlad ang iyong lakas, disiplina, at kumpiyansa sa isang suportadong kapaligiran. Makipagsanay sa mga bihasang tagapagturo, pagbutihin ang iyong kakayahan, at maging bahagi ng isang masiglang komunidad. Huwag palampasin ang pagkakataong ito—sumali na at ipamalas ang iyong tunay na potensyal.
KYORUGI
Kyorugi is the combat-oriented aspect of Taekwondo, where practitioners engage in controlled sparring matches. It emphasizes speed, strategy, and precision, requiring fighters to anticipate their opponent's moves and react accordingly.
  • Learning techniques in motion – Practicing basic movements and footwork
  • Yaksok Kyorugi (Patternized Sparring) – Training in set attack and defense sequences to understand timing and distance.
  • Jayu Kyorugi (Free Sparring) – Applying techniques in real-time combat situations, adapting to an opponent's movements.
POOMSAE
Poomsae is the technical and artistic side of Taekwondo, consisting of choreographed movements that simulate combat scenarios. It helps practitioners refine their techniques, balance, and breathing control.
  • Memorizing movement sequences – Understanding the significance of each form.
  • Practicing footwork and stances – Ensuring proper balance and execution.
  • Controlling speed and force– Adjusting movements for precision and effectiveness.
