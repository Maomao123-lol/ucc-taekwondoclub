ABOUT US
MISSION
The University of Caloocan City Taekwondo Club is dedicated to developing skilled, disciplined, and respectful athletes through martial arts training. We do not train to harm others, but to cultivate sportsmanship, self-control, and personal growth. Our practice instills perseverance, humility, and teamwork, empowering students to represent UCC with integrity, excellence, and respect both in competition and in life.
VISION
To be a thriving and principled Taekwondo community, where athletes train with purpose, discipline, and respect. We aim to shape individuals who embody honor, fairness, and continuous self-improvement, inspiring students to use Taekwondo's teachings as a foundation for confidence, responsibility, and leadership in all aspects of life.
